Mortality Minder

In the group of RPI IDEA; Supervised by Dr. Bennett


This summer, I joined RPI IDEA to conduct the research about Machine Learning and data processing, and the topic of our project is mortality minder. In this project, our team mainly focus on impute the missing data. We tried several different methods: Neural- Network, K-neareast neighbor, random forest, SVM, etc. And we use selp-modified Amelia package in R to impute the missing values.

We also using multiple machine learning methods to find the most important social determinants related to different cause of mortality. (Death of Despair, Cardiovascular Disease, Cancer,etc.)

Auto Life-Safer


This project is for MCM 2019, we chose Problem B and we aimed to design the best pattern to relief disaster by sending the drones. We used basic quadratic panelty to minimize the cost, and we designed two models for different arrangement for ISO cargo containers, and use bin-packing model for the arrangement of drones in each container.

Driving on Electric Vehicles


This project is for MCM 2018, we chose Problem D and we analyze the growth pattern of Tesla in urban and rural areas using the basic statistic models. We built the micro model for driving on a straight road and investigate the gap between two supercharger by simulating the e-consumption and performance of e-cars. And we also built a macro model for total number of supercharger in different areas of the contry.
The problem statement is here , and our paper is here .
